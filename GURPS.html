Everyman Skills

Please include these skills on your character sheet, even if you put 0 CPs into the skill. Its not uncommon to need the level of these skills for the entire party.

  • Brawling
  • Climbing
  • First Aid (Human)
  • Hiking
  • Jummping
  • Navigation (Land)
  • Riding (Equines)
  • Search
  • Survival (Woodlands)
  • Swimming
  • Traps

    • Wizard Spells

    Wizards in DF must follow the spell restrictions below. There are some eceptions, for details see DF01 pg. 20.

    • Animal, Enchantment, Plant, Healing (except Lend Energy and Recover Energy), Radiation, Weather colleges are not allowed.
    • Any spells that allow teleportaion or time manipulation are not allowed.
    • Enlarge, Invisibilty and any spells that Create thing have additional rules.
    • NPCs will have taken precautions against any spells that would shortcut an adventure. E.g. scry prevention for an information gathering adventure.
